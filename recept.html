Mexican Casserole

Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Sauté chopped onions, garlic, carrots, and zucchini in olive oil until soft. Stir in organic crushed tomatoes, basil, salt, and pepper; simmer for 10 minutes. Layer whole-grain pasta sheets, vegetable sauce, and ricotta cheese in a baking dish. Repeat layers, finishing with mozzarella on top. Bake for 30–35 minutes until golden and bubbling.

Fresh Garden Salad

Toss mixed organic greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber slices, and shredded carrots in a bowl. Drizzle with a dressing made from olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, and honey. Sprinkle with toasted sunflower seeds before serving.

oasted Beet and Quinoa Salad

Roast peeled organic beets at 200°C (400°F) for 30–40 minutes, then dice. Cook organic quinoa and let it cool. Mix quinoa, beets, arugula, goat cheese, and walnuts. Dress with balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper.

Fluffy Organic Pancakes

In a bowl, whisk together organic whole wheat flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. In another bowl, mix almond milk, a flaxseed egg (or organic egg), and maple syrup. Combine wet and dry ingredients; stir until just combined. Cook small scoops on a hot, greased pan until bubbles form; flip and cook until golden.

Rustic Apple Hand Pie

Slice organic apples thinly; toss with cinnamon, nutmeg, and a touch of maple syrup. Roll out whole-grain pastry dough; cut into circles. Place apple mixture in the center; fold and seal edges. Brush with almond milk and sprinkle with coconut sugar. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 20–25 minutes until golden.

Savory Spinach and Mushroom Tart

Sauté organic spinach, mushrooms, and onions until soft; season with salt, pepper, and thyme. Roll out spelt flour dough into a tart pan. Spread a thin layer of goat cheese, then the spinach-mushroom mixture on top. Bake at 190°C (375°F) for 25–30 minutes until edges are crisp.
